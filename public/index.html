Possible doubts

How do I classify my physical activity?

  • Sedentary: few or no exercises.
  • Lightly active: practice some sport 1 or 3 days per week.
  • Moderately active: practice sport sports 3 or 5 days per week.
  • Very active: practice some sport 6 or 7 days per week.
  • Extra active: practice a very hard sport constantly.

Calories calculation

If you’re curious about how we come to those results that you saw ahead when you used the calculator, here is the used formulas to get those results:

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) formulas

  • 10 x weight(kg) + 6.25 x height(cm) - 5 x age + 5 for man.
  • 10 x weight(kg) + 6.25 x height(cm) - 5 x age - 161 for ​woman.

Determining your activity level according to your physical activity

  • Sedentary: 1,2.
  • Lightly active: 1,375.
  • Moderately active: 1,55.
  • Very active: 1,725.
  • Extra active: 1,9.

So, the formula to get the needed calories to...

  • Maintain your weight is: BMR x activity level.
  • Lose weight is: (BMR x activity level) - 450.
  • Gain weight is: (BMR x activity level) + 450.
